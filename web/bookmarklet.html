LinkedIn Quick-Add

Save any LinkedIn job to your tracker in one click — without leaving the page.

Step 1 — Install

Drag this button to your bookmarks bar:

bookmark_add Save to Golem OS

Can't drag? Right-click the button → "Bookmark this link"

Step 2 — Use it

1

Open any LinkedIn job posting

Works on individual job pages (linkedin.com/jobs/view/…)

2

Click "Save to Golem OS" in your bookmarks bar

A small window opens with the job pre-filled

3

Review the details, set a status, hit Save

The window closes automatically — you're back on LinkedIn

What gets captured

work Job title
business Company
link Job URL
flag Status (Wishlist)

You can edit any field in the popup before saving.
